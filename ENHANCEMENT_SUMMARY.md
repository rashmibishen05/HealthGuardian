### Health Calculators
1. **BMI Calculator**
   - Input: Height, Weight
   - Output: BMI score + category
   - Categories: Underweight, Normal, Overweight, Obese

2. **BMR Calculator**
   - Input: Age, Height, Weight, Gender
   - Output: Daily calorie burn
   - Uses Mifflin-St Jeor equation

3. **Water Intake**
   - Input: Weight
   - Output: Daily liters needed
   - Formula: 33ml per kg
